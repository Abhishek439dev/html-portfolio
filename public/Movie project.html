The Best Movies according to Abhishek

My top three movies of all time

Idiocracy

This is really a great sci-fi comedy movie. It shows that humans have evolve into a very low iq living organisms and they are unable to solve their basic problems.
In this period a normal human with average iq of current period enters that solves all the world problems and become president of hat country.

Downsizing

In this movie we can see that the world has developed a lot such that the living cost has increased too much.
To overcome this scientists have evolved a method which reduces the size o human body so that we consume less resources and can survive better.

Maze runner

This movie shows that their are a group of human beings living in a forest area learning to survive from scratch. Lateron its discovered that they all were trapped in a chamber and an experiment was being performed upon them to find out the fittest one among them.
They are performing this experiment to fight a deadly disease spread in the outer world.
